ABOUT US

   Each of us has repeatedly been in a situation where you urgently need to find out some information, but you don’t know how to formulate your request in order to get the desired result. This fact is the main reason for creating this product. "WhatACatDog" aims to help lovers of animals (in particular cats and dogs) and just people who want to find out the breed or additional information about a particular representative of the cat or dog family.

In addition to the basic recognition function on the website, you can get:

Telegram bot for instant cat/dog breed identifcation.
Optional web registration with the possibility of authorization through gmail account to get the best selection of cats and dogs.
If you are registered, the history of your requests will be saved in your account.
Monthly newsletter statistics in Telegram of the most requested breeds.
Weekly newsletter in Telegram of a selection of cats and dogs, based on users’ preferences.
